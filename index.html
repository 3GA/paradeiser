features

  • small and easy to use
  • important links instantly visible and clickable (beat this u little piece of meat)
  • subtle design, easily customizable through SCSS
  • Overflow menu for less important links (yes, this is basically a hamburger)
  • compatible with all major frameworks including Bootstrap, Foundation and PureCSS
  • may easily be used as a mobile nav only, keep your standard nav on desktop
  • hide links based on device size (see paradeiser-hidden css classes in demo3.html)
  • Won't kill your favorite GOT characters
